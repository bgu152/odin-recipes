Fried pike

Description

This is a simple recipe for Northern Pike (Esox Lucius).

If you catch the fish yourself, it is important to drain it of blood first. Then gut it and slice it into fillets. The fillets are fried in butter in a frying pan on a hot stove. Season with salt and white pepper. The fillets must be well done.

When the fish is fried, squeese fresh lime or lemon over it. Serve with potatoes and sallad. Horseradish paste may be used for additional seasoning and spicyness.

Ingredients

  • One northern pike
  • Salt
  • White pepper
  • Horseradish paste
  • Lime or lemon
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gut fish and slice into fillets
  2. Salt the fillets
  3. Fry the fillets in butter on a hot stove
  4. Add white pepper
  5. Fry until well done
  6. Squeese lemon or lime on fish
  7. Serve with potatoes and salad
  8. Add horseradish paste for extra umpf
